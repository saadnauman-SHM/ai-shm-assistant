Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) is used to monitor the condition of structures such as bridges and buildings.

SHM systems use sensors like accelerometers and strain gauges to measure vibrations and stress.

Cracks and corrosion in structures are indicators of potential structural failure.

Temperature changes can affect the integrity of materials and must be monitored.

SHM helps in early detection of damage to prevent catastrophic failures.

Bridge monitoring systems use vibration analysis to detect anomalies.

Machine learning models can be used to analyze SHM data.

Sensors continuously collect data for real-time structural assessment.
